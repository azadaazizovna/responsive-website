Courses We Offer

The courses offered by this degree are suited to the opportunities available in both the private and public sectors.

Computing Security

The Master of Science in Computing Security at Rochester Institute of Technology gives students an understanding of the technological and ethical roles of computing security in today's society and its importance across the breadth of computing disciplines.

Electrical Engineering

Rapidly developing global technologies are changing the way we live. The Master of Science (MS) in Electrical Engineering at RIT Dubai combines theoretical fundamentals and practical applications in this dynamic field and educates students in the practices.

Mechanical Engineering

The Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering is a 30-credit hour degree program. The program allocates twenty-four credit hours of course work, distributed in the core, focus area, and elective groups, and six credit hours of thesis work.

Our Global Campus
